PaisoPulse – Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address solely to identify your account and sync your data across devices. We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate you securely via email/password or Google Sign-In
  • Sync your expense entries and budgets across your devices
  • Send a password reset email if you request one

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – authentication and database (privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In – optional OAuth login (privacy policy)

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at niyaz47nhh@gmail.com. We will process the request within 7 days.

6. Children's Privacy

PaisoPulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The effective date at the top will reflect any changes.
